Sponsor Us

The team depends on the generous support of its sponsors and could not exist without them. Without funding from sponsors, we wouldn't be able to continue to broaden our STEM experiences for future students. To become a sponsor, we ask for monetary or hardware contributions to support our efforts in advancing autonomous underwater robotics technology.

We partner with our non-profit 501(c)(3) Pleasanton Unified School District which provides education programs for 15 schools in Pleasanton, so your monetary donations are tax-deductible.
